Book Writing
and Publishing Has Never Been This Easy

Ever dreamt of seeing your name on a book cover? At Roosevelt Writes, we're here to make that dream a reality. Unlike the frustration of searching for a "find a book publisher" solution, we offer an easy process that guides you every step of the way, from the initial concept to becoming a published author.

1. Initial Consultation

Our collaboration starts with thoroughly discussing your vision for the book. This includes understanding your goals, preferences, and expectations. We'll then explore the genre, target audience, and desired tone to ensure that we align our efforts with your vision.

2. Outline Approval

Once we understand your requirements clearly, our team will draft an extensive outline for your approval. This outline would be the guideline for the entire project, outlining the structure, key themes, and chapter breakdown. We welcome your feedback and revisions to ensure the outline matches your vision.

3. Writing Your Story

With the outline approved, our talented writers get to work on bringing your story to life. Using their expertise and creativity, they write engaging prose that captivates readers from the first page to the last. Throughout the writing process, we maintain open communication to address any questions or concerns that may arise.

4. Editing and Proofreading

Once the initial draft is complete, our experienced editors vigilantly review the manuscript for clarity, coherence, and consistency. They ensure that your story flows seamlessly and that the language is perfectly polished. We also conduct thorough proofreading to catch any grammatical errors or typos before proceeding to the next step.

5. Book Formatting

With the content finalized, we will format the book for publication. Our team ensures that the layout is professional and visually appealing, optimizing the formatting for both print and digital formats. We pay close attention to details such as font selection, spacing, and alignment to enhance the reader experience.

6. Book Publishing

Once the formatting is complete, we handle the publishing process on your behalf. Whether you choose traditional publishing or self-publishing, we handle all the logistics to ensure a smooth transition from manuscript to published book. We guide you through each step of the publishing process, from ISBN assignment to distribution.

7. Book Marketing

After publishing the book, we help you create a customized marketing strategy to promote your work to the widest possible audience. Our marketing experts use various channels, including social media, email marketing, and book launch events, to generate buzz and drive sales.

8. Designing Covers

Finally, we collaborate with talented designers to create eye-catching covers that capture the essence of your book. Our designers work closely with you to develop concepts that reflect your vision and resonate with your target audience. We ensure that the cover design stands out on bookshelves and online platforms, enticing readers to pick up your book.
